Instructions that Indicate How to Use the Toolbar to the Left

Most of the library's DOM manipulation occurs during end-user interactions, so I'm giving you instructions to let you know how to use it!

  • You can turn the Highlighter and Notetaker on/off via the switches under the “Highlighter” and “Notetaker” sections respectively, available in the toolbar. (It won't let you turn both on.)
  • When the Highlighter is on, you can highlight text anywhere on the page by selecting it.
  • When the “Notetaker” is on, you can paste notes at any location on the page by clicking. (Note that the Notetaker turns itself off once a note is pasted.) You can customize the note's text by typing in the "Note Content" section.
